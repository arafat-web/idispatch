The passenger transportation industry has evolved.

Yet many limousine and executive car companies still operate dispatch under outdated models — fragmented staffing, limited coverage, reactive communication, and inconsistent client handling.

The Problem with
Traditional Dispatch Models

Many transportation companies struggle with one or more of the following:

  • * Missed inbound calls during peak hours
  • * Inconsistent call quality and booking procedures
  • * Overworked in-house dispatchers
  • * Limited after-hours coverage
  • * Poor coordination between office and chauffeurs
  • * Lack of reporting and performance tracking
  • * Reactive problem-solving instead of proactive trip management

Traditional answering services lack industry knowledge. Software-only solutions lack human judgment. In-house staffing creates scalability limitations.

Modern transportation companies require a hybrid model:

Technology-driven. Human-managed. Operationally disciplined.

That is the IDispatch standard.

What We Do

IDispatch Solutions operates as a structured extension of your internal operations.

Our team integrates directly into your dispatch platform, follows your pricing rules, service policies, and brand voice, and manages real-time reservation and dispatch functions with precision.

Core Operational Functions

Live Call Monitoring & Professional Answering
All inbound calls answered under your company name with a trained, industry-specific script and corporate communication standard.

Reservation Processing & Booking Management
New bookings entered accurately into your dispatch software. Quote generation based on your rate structure. Reservation confirmations, modifications, cancellations handled seamlessly.

Chauffeur Coordination & Trip Oversight
Real-time driver communication. Trip status monitoring. Flight tracking. Proactive issue management. Escalation protocols when needed.

After-Hours & 24/7 Coverage — Nights, weekends, holidays, overflow support without interruption.

Affiliate Trip Handling — Structured coordination for affiliate bookings, confirmations, and communication standards aligned with professional network expectations.
