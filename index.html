Frequently Asked Questions

Is your service really 24/7?

Yes, we provide round-the-clock live dispatching every single day of the year, including holidays. Our teams work in rotating shifts to ensure peak performance at any hour.

Do you use automated voices?

No. Every call is answered by a trained, professional human dispatcher who acts as a direct extension of your team. We believe human touch is critical for premium service.

Can you handle large fleets?

Absolutely. Our Enterprise Infrastructure Package is specifically designed for high-volume operators with multi-location fleets. We provide a dedicated dispatch team, SOP implementation, and scalable resources to match your fleet size.

Will I have a dedicated manager?

Yes. Every client receives a dedicated account manager who learns your fleet's nuances, driver preferences, and operational procedures — serving as your single point of contact for all dispatch-related matters.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is volume-based and transparent. We offer tiered models — Lite, Growth, Enterprise, and Global Elite — based on monthly trip counts and complexity. Multi-city and multi-timezone operations receive custom quotes. Book a consultation to get a proposal tailored to your fleet.
